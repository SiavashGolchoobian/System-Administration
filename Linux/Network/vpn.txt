--Use Cisco AnyConnect as vpn tools, Replace vpnserver.com with your server address (for example cuk.dnsfind.net:1397), you can get list of all cisco vpn servers from www.turbov.net/connections.html or by reviewing your turbovpn email content that including list of servers.
sudo /sbin/modprobe tun
sudo apt-get install openconnect
sudo openconnect vpnserver.com
--Type your vpn user name and password
--Open a new terminal with same linux account as previous and do your work
--For disconnectig from vpn press Ctrl+Z on VPN terminal
